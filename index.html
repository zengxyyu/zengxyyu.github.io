Biography

Hello! I am a third-year MSc student at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC). I conduct research at Laboratory of Intelligent Collaborative Computing, under the supervision of Prof. Jie Lin.

My primary research interests include Deep Learning and Artificial Intelligence, mainly focusing on LLM (Large Language Model), AGI (Artificial General Intelligence) and CV (Computer Vision).

Research Area

  • LLM and LLM-based Agent (e.g., Transformers with memory)
  • Computer Vision (e.g., Vit with memory)
  • Multimodal (e.g., CLIP and DALL·E)
  • Artificial Intelligence and its Applications (e.g., Medical Images)
